Work report

I report my recent works, including the following:

  1. Sturm-Liouville problem on 1D finite domain, with Dirichlet or Neumann boundary conditions.
  2. Generalized eigenproblem of 1D Laplace opretor on complete binary tree.
  3. FEM calculate of those specific 1D problems.

My advisor point out that I should focus on the following:

  1. I should consider the eigenvalue problem from a weak formulation standpoint or physical standpoint.
  2. I should maintain written documentations of recent works or ideas.
  3. When deriving the theory, I should proceed step by step, without skipping or omitting any steps, and be as detailed as possible.

Also I’m asked to do the following:

  1. Write a report about the recent works biweekly.
  2. Extend Sturm-Liouville problem to 2D and higher dimensions on $[0,1]^d$, with Dirichlet or Neumann boundary conditions. Calculate it using Firedrake, and compare the results with analytical solutions.
  3. Find the analytical solution of the QG eigenproblem on the complete binary tree, and compare it with the numerical solution.
  4. Read the papers about the FEM on QG and other numercal methods for the eigenproblem.
