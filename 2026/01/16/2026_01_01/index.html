发表于 2026-01-16 分类于 life

Reflection

I went through many changes last year.

I made the decision to begin my postgraduate life, and I followed through.
I earned my bachelor’s degree and stepped into a new stage that I had imagined for a long time,
but did not fully understand. Researchin numerical PDEs, turned out to be much harder than I expected.
Often, I feel unsure of where I am going or whether I am doing things the right way.

For the past four months, my life has mostly revolved around the college routine:
taking courses, rebuilding my foundation, and preparing for final exams. The days felt repetitive,
but also strangely grounding. I met new people—some serious, some very different from those
I knew before. Being around them made me more aware of my own limits and habits， and my easy personality.

I do feel that I have changed, though perhaps not in the way I hoped.
I am still cautious, still afraid of change, and sometimes afraid of making the wrong move.
I want to make friends, yet I fear the complications that come with closeness.
At times, I try to convince myself that I am comfortable, even when I’m not sure whether that comfort is real or something
I am pretending to feel.

Can I change? I don’t have an answer yet. But maybe making this blog is already a small beginning.

Hello,2026

My first and greatest challenge is the PhD qualifying examination. And I should try my best to learn how
to study ,how to find some questions. Plus, I should make some friends, althrough I still dont know how.

Side quests：learn to live myself, find my own life, try to love.
